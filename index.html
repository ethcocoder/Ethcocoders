About Ethco Coders

Ethco Coders is a dynamic collective of four visionary Ethiopian software engineers, founded in 2025 with a mission to solve real-world problems through elegant, scalable, and ethical technology. Rooted in Ethiopia's rich culture of resilience and innovation, we bring fresh perspectives, cutting-edge technical skills, and unwavering dedication to every project we undertake.

We believe that software is not just about functionality—it's about empowerment, accessibility, and impact. As young African technologists, we are committed to building digital solutions that uplift communities, drive local entrepreneurship, and position Ethiopia as a rising hub of tech excellence on the global stage.
